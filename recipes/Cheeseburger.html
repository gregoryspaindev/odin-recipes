Cheeseburger

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Chuck
  • Seasoned Salt
  • Pepper
  • American Cheese
  • Buns

Steps

  1. Preheat grill or griddle.
  2. Gently form ground beef into four equal patties.
  3. Season patties generously with the seasoned salt and pepper on both sides.
  4. Grill patties for 5 minutes, then flip.
  5. Top the patties with your desired amount of American Cheese.
  6. Continue to grill until cheese melts and patties reach your desired temperature.
  7. Place buns on grill to toast while patties finish cooking.
  8. Remove patties from grill and place on toasted buns.
  9. Top with your preferred condiments, and enjoy!
