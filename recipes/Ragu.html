Ragu

Ingredients

  • 1 Medium-sized Ham shank
  • 2 lg cans Whole, Peeled Tomatoes
  • 1 lg can Tomato Puree
  • 1/4 c. Tomato Paste
  • 1/4 c. Olive Oil
  • 6 cloves Garlic, sliced paper thin
  • 1 lg Yellow Onion, roughly diced
  • 1 tbsp Whole Fennel Seed
  • Oregano (to taste)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Roughly chop whole tomatoes, preserving any liquid but discarding the seeds.
  2. Lightly toast fennel in small frying pan over medium heat.
  3. In large stockpot, heat oil to a medium heat.
  4. Add onions, lightly salt them, and cook until just soft.
  5. Add garlic and fennel seed to pan and reduce heat, letting garlic nearly dissolve.
  6. Increase heat to medium-high and gradually add tomatoes, then the puree and paste.
  7. Add salt, pepper, and oregano. Taste throughout cooking and adjust seasoning as necessary.
  8. Add ham shank to pot.
  9. Reduce heat to low and cook several hours, until shank is falling apart.
