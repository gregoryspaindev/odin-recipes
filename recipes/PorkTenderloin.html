Marinated Pork Tenderloin

Ingredients

  • 2 Pork Tenderloins
  • 1/2 c. Worcestershire Sauce
    • /
  • 1/4 c. Tamari (or Soy) Sauce
  • Dijon Mustard (enough to coat pork)
  • 1/4 c. Garlic, minced
  • Black Pepper (to taste)

Steps

Marinade:

  1. Dry pork with paper towels.
  2. Rub pork all over with the Dijon Mustard.
  3. Spread the garlic all over Pork.
  4. Season pork with pepper to taste.
  5. Use a fork to poke many holes in the pork.
  6. Pour Worcestershire sauce and Tamari into a large ziper bag.
  7. Carefully place pork in bag with sauces.
  8. Place in refridgerator for 2 to 24 hours.
  1. Preheat grill to 500 degrees.
  2. Lightly shake excess marinade from pork.
  3. Place pork over direct heat to sear, flipping every 90 sec.
  4. Once desired sear is attained, adjust grill so pork is no longer over direct heat.
  5. Cook approx. 25 minutes until thickest part of the Tenderloin registers 145 degrees on an instant-read thermometer.
  6. Remove pork from heat, and let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
  7. To serve, slice pork thinly against the grain.
